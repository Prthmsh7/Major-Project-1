## Key Features

### **Voice-First "Hands-Free" Interface**
* **Core Function:** Your assistant listens and responds to voice commands, allowing for complete hands-free operation while cooking.
* **Wake Word Activation:** Say "Hey Assistant" to activate listening mode.
* **Conversational Commands:** Ask naturally, "What can I make with these tomatoes?" or "What's the next step?"
* **Audible Cooking Guidance:** Your assistant reads recipe instructions aloud, step-by-step.
* **Voice-Controlled Timers:** Effortlessly manage your cooking times by saying, "Set a 10-minute timer for the pasta."

### **AI-Powered Recipe Generation**
* **Core Function:** The core intelligence of your assistant, using Gemini AI to generate unique recipes from the ingredients you already have.
* **Pantry-Based Suggestions:** Select items from your digital pantry, and your assistant will create a complete recipe with instructions, cooking time, and more.
* **Advanced Customization:** Filter recipe generation by dietary needs (vegan, keto), cuisine type, available time, and even the appliances you own.
* **"Surprise Me!" Feature:** Let your assistant get creative with a single click for an unexpected and delicious meal idea.
* **Personalized Taste Profile:** By rating recipes, you teach your assistant your preferences, leading to even better suggestions over time.

### **Smart Pantry Management**
* **Core Function:** Your assistant keeps a perfect, real-time digital inventory of your kitchen, so you always know what you have.
* **Effortless Item Entry:** Add items quickly via barcode scanning (using the browser camera), voice commands ("Add milk to the pantry"), or manual entry.
* **Automatic Ingredient Depletion:** Your assistant automatically asks to remove ingredients from your inventory after you've cooked a meal.
* **Low-Stock & Expiration Alerts:** Receive notifications when you're running low on staples or when an item is nearing its expiration date.

### **Comprehensive Nutrition Tracking**
* **Core Function:** Your assistant helps you achieve your health goals by seamlessly tracking your nutritional intake.
* **Personalized Daily Goals:** Set targets for calories, macros (protein, carbs, fats), and other nutrients.
* **One-Tap Meal Logging:** Log any meal made with your assistant's recipes with a single tap, automatically capturing all nutritional data.
* **Visual Progress Dashboards:** View beautiful charts and graphs that show your progress towards your goals on a daily and weekly basis.

### **Intelligent Shopping Lists**
* **Core Function:** Your assistant automates shopping list creation, ensuring you never forget an essential ingredient again.
* **Auto-Generated Lists:** When you decide on a recipe, your assistant instantly creates a list of only the ingredients you're missing.
* **Smart Aisle Categorization:** The shopping list is automatically sorted by grocery store aisle (Produce, Dairy, etc.) to make your trip faster.
* **Shareable & Collaborative:** Share the list with family members; updates sync in real-time as items are checked off.

### **Universal Progressive Web App (PWA)**
* **Core Function:** The foundation of your assistant, providing a fast, reliable, and engaging experience on any platform.
* **Works Everywhere:** Access your assistant on your phone, tablet, or desktop computer through any web browser.
* **No App Store Required:** Start using it immediately by visiting its URL.
